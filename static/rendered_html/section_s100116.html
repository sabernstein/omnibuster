SEC. 100116. PUBLICITY.   In carrying out the MBDA Business Center Program, the Under Secretary shall widely publicize the MBDA Business Center Program, including— (1) on the website of the Agency; (2) via social media outlets; and (3) by sharing information relating to the MBDA Business Center Program with community-based organizations, including interpretation groups where necessary, to communicate in the most common languages spoken by the groups served by those organizations.
