SEC. 2603. FUNDING FOR NURSE CORPS.   In addition to amounts otherwise available, there is appropriated to the Secretary for fiscal year 2021, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $200,000,000, to remain available until expended, for carrying out section 846 of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 297n).
