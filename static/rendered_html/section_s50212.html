SEC. 50212. GRANTS TO ALASKA TO IMPROVE SANITATION IN RURAL AND NATIVE VILLAGES.   Section 303 of the Safe Drinking Water Act Amendments of 1996 (33 U.S.C. 1263a) is amended—(1) in subsection (b), by striking “50 percent” and inserting “75 percent”; and (2) in subsection (e), by striking “this section” and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting the following: “this section—“(1) $40,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2022 through 2024; “(2) $50,000,000 for fiscal year 2025; and “(3) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2026.” .
