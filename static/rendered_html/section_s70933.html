SEC. 70933. SENSE OF CONGRESS ON BUYING AMERICAN.   It is the sense of Congress that—(1) every executive agency should maximize, through terms and conditions of Federal financial assistance awards and Federal procurements, the use of goods, products, and materials produced in the United States and contracts for outsourced government service contracts to be performed by United States nationals; (2) every executive agency should scrupulously monitor, enforce, and comply with Buy American laws, to the extent they apply, and minimize the use of waivers; and (3) every executive agency should use available data to routinely audit its compliance with Buy American laws.
