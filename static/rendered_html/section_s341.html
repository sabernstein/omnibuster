SEC. 341. DUPLICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR ECONOMIC INJURY DISASTER LOAN RECIPIENTS.   Section 7(a)(36)(Q) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 636(a)(36)(Q)) is amended by striking “ during the period beginning on January 31, 2020, and ending on the date on which covered loans are made available”.
