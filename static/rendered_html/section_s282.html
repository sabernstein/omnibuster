SEC. 282. OVERSIGHT AND AUDIT REPORTING.   Section 19010(a)(1) of the CARES Act is amended by striking “ and” at the end of subparagraph (F), by striking “ and” at the end of subparagraph (G), and by adding at the end the following new subparagraphs: “(H) the Committee on Finance of the Senate; and “(I) the Committee on Ways and Means of the House of Representatives; and” .
