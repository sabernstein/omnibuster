SEC. 22426. RAILROAD POINT OF CONTACT FOR PUBLIC SAFETY ISSUES.   All railroads shall—(1) provide railroad contact information for public safety issues, including a telephone number, to the relevant Federal, State, and local oversight agencies; and (2) post the information described in paragraph (1) on a publicly accessible website.
