SEC. 129. ENSURING APPROPRIATE RESPONSIBILITY OF AIRCRAFT CERTIFICATION AND FLIGHT STANDARDS PERFORMANCE OBJECTIVES AND METRICS. (a) Repeals.— Sections 211 and 221 of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 ( 49 U.S.C. 44701 note) are repealed. (b) Conforming Repeals.— Paragraphs (8) and (9) of section 202(c) of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 ( 49 U.S.C. 44701 note) are repealed.
