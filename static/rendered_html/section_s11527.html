SEC. 11527. BLOOD TRANSPORT VEHICLES.  Section 166(b) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:“(6) Blood transport vehicles.—The public authority may allow blood transport vehicles that are transporting blood between a collection point and a hospital or storage center to use the HOV facility if the public authority establishes requirements for clearly identifying such vehicles.” .
