Section s3832

SEC. 3832. DIABETES PROGRAMS.(a) Type I.—Section 330B(b)(2)(D) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 254c–2(b)(2)(D)) is amended by striking “and 2019, and $96,575,342 for the period beginning on October 1, 2019, and ending on May 22, 2020” and inserting “through 2020, and $25,068,493 for the period beginning on October 1, 2020, and ending on November 30, 2020”. (b) Indians.—Section 330C(c)(2)(D) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 254c–3(c)(2)(D)) is amended by striking “and 2019, and $96,575,342 for the period beginning on October 1, 2019, and ending on May 22, 2020” and inserting “through 2020, and $25,068,493 for the period beginning on October 1, 2020, and ending on November 30, 2020”.
