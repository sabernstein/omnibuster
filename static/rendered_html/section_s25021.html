SEC. 25021. INTERMODAL TRANSPORTATION ADVISORY BOARD REPEAL.(a) In General.—Section 5502 of title 49, United States Code, is repealed. (b) Clerical Amendment.—The analysis for subchapter I of chapter 55 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking the item relating to section 5502.
