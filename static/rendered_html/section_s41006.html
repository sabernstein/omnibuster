SEC. 41006. WATER POWER PROJECTS. (a) Hydropower and Marine Energy.— There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary— (1) to carry out activities under section 634 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 ( 42 U.S.C. 17213), $36,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2022 through 2025; and (2) to carry out activities under section 635 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 ( 42 U.S.C. 17214), $70,400,000 for the period of fiscal years 2022 through 2025. (b) National Marine Energy Centers.— There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out activities under section 636 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 ( 42 U.S.C. 17215) $40,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2022 through 2025.
