SEC. 11124. CONSOLIDATION OF PROGRAMS.   Section 1519(a) of MAP–21 ( Public Law 112–141; 126 Stat. 574; 129 Stat. 1423) is amended, in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by striking “ fiscal years 2016 through 2020” and inserting “ fiscal years 2022 through 2026”.
