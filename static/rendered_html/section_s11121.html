SEC. 11121. CONSTRUCTION OF FERRY BOATS AND FERRY TERMINAL FACILITIES.  Section 147 of title 23, United States Code, is amended by striking subsection (h) and inserting the following:“(h) Authorization of Appropriations.—There are authorized to be appropriated out of the Highway Trust Fund (other than the Mass Transit Account) to carry out this section—“(1) $110,000,000 for fiscal year 2022; “(2) $112,000,000 for fiscal year 2023; “(3) $114,000,000 for fiscal year 2024; “(4) $116,000,000 for fiscal year 2025; and “(5) $118,000,000 for fiscal year 2026.” .
