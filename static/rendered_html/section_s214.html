SEC. 214. UPPER MISSISSIPPI RIVER COMPREHENSIVE PLAN. (a) Assessment.— The Secretary shall conduct an assessment of the water resources needs of the Upper Mississippi River under section 729 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1986 ( 33 U.S.C. 2267a). (b) Requirements.— The Secretary shall carry out the assessment under subsection (a) in accordance with the requirements in section 1206(b) of Water Resources Development Act of 2016 ( 130 Stat. 1686).
