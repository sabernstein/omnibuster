SEC. 23002. MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY ADVISORY COMMITTEE.   Section 4144 of the SAFETEA–LU (49 U.S.C. 31100 note; Public Law 109–59) is amended—(1) in subsection (b)(1), in the second sentence, by inserting “, including small business motor carriers” after “industry”; and (2) in subsection (d), by striking “September 30, 2013” and inserting “September 30, 2025”.
