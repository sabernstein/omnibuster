SEC. 302. DEFINITIONS.   In this Act: (1) Administration; administrator.— The terms “Administration” and “Administrator” mean the Small Business Administration and the Administrator thereof, respectively. (2) Small business concern.— The term “ small business concern” has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 632).
