capitol complex health and safety Sec. 212. In addition to the amounts appropriated under this Act under the heading “ Office of the Attending Physician”, there is hereby appropriated to the Office of the Attending Physician $5,000,000, to remain available until expended, for response to COVID–19, including testing, subject to the same terms and conditions as the amounts appropriated under such heading.
