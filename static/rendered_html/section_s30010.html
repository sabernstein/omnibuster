SEC. 30010. GENERAL PROVISIONS. Section 5323(u) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking paragraph (2) and inserting the following: “(2) Exception.— For purposes of paragraph (1), the term ‘ otherwise related legally or financially’ does not include— “(A) a minority relationship or investment; or “(B) relationship with or investment in a subsidiary, joint venture, or other entity based in a country described in paragraph (1)(B) that does not export rolling stock or components of rolling stock for use in the United States.” .
