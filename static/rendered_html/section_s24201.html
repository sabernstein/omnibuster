SEC. 24201. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.   There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out chapter 301, and part C of subtitle VI, of title 49, United States Code— (1) $200,294,333 for fiscal year 2022; (2) $204,300,219 for fiscal year 2023; (3) $208,386,224 for fiscal year 2024; (4) $212,553,948 for fiscal year 2025; and (5) $216,805,027 for fiscal year 2026.
