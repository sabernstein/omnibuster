Section s3703

SEC. 3703. INCREASING MEDICARE TELEHEALTH FLEXIBILITIES DURING EMERGENCY PERIOD.   Section 1135 of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1320b–5) is amended—(1) in subsection (b)(8), by striking “to an individual by a qualified provider (as defined in subsection (g)(3))” and all that follows through the period and inserting “, the requirements of section 1834(m).”; and (2) in subsection (g), by striking paragraph (3).
