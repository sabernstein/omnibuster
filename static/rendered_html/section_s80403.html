SEC. 80403. INCREASE IN NATIONAL LIMITATION AMOUNT FOR QUALIFIED HIGHWAY OR SURFACE FREIGHT TRANSPORTATION FACILITIES.(a) In General.—Section 142(m)(2)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking “$15,000,000,000” and inserting “$30,000,000,000”. (b) Effective Date.—The amendment made by this section shall apply to bonds issued after the date of the enactment of this Act.
