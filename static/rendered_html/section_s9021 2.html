SEC. 9021. EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE FOR STATES WITH ADVANCES.   Section 1202(b)(10)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1322(b)(10)(A)) is amended by striking “ March 14, 2021” and inserting “ September 6, 2021”.
