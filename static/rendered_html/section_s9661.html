SEC. 9661. IMPROVING AFFORDABILITY BY EXPANDING PREMIUM ASSISTANCE FOR CONSUMERS. (b) Conforming Amendment.— Section 36B(c)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph: “(E) Temporary rule for 2021 and 2022.— In the case of a taxable year beginning in 2021 or 2022, subparagraph (A) shall be applied without regard to ‘but does not exceed 400 percent’.” . (c) Effective Date.— The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2020.
