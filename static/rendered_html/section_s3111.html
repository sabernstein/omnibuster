Section s3111

SEC. 3111. PRIORITIZE REVIEWS OF DRUG APPLICATIONS; INCENTIVES.   Section 506C(g) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. 356c(g)) is amended—(1) in paragraph (1), by striking “the Secretary may” and inserting “the Secretary shall, as appropriate”; (2) in paragraph (1), by inserting “prioritize and” before “expedite the review”; and (3) in paragraph (2), by inserting “prioritize and” before “expedite an inspection”.
