SEC. 11317. CATEGORICAL EXCLUSION FOR PROJECTS OF LIMITED FEDERAL ASSISTANCE.   Section 1317(1) of MAP–21 (23 U.S.C. 109 note; Public Law 112–141) is amended—(1) in subparagraph (A), by striking “$5,000,000” and inserting “$6,000,000”; and (2) in subparagraph (B), by striking “$30,000,000” and inserting “$35,000,000”.
