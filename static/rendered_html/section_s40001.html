SEC. 40001. DEFINITIONS.   In this division: (1) Department.— The term “ Department” means the Department of Energy. (2) Indian tribe.— The term “ Indian Tribe” has the meaning given the term in section 4 of the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act ( 25 U.S.C. 5304). (3) Secretary.— The term “ Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy.
