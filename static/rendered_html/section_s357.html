SEC. 357. LAKE EUFAULA ADVISORY COMMITTEE.   Section 3133(b) of the Water Resources Development Act of 2007 ( 121 Stat. 1141) is amended by adding at the end the following: “(5) Termination.— The committee shall terminate on the date that is 30 days after the date on which the committee submits final recommendations to the Secretary.” .
