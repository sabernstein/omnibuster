SEC. 24107. MINIMUM PENALTIES FOR REPEAT OFFENDERS FOR DRIVING WHILE INTOXICATED OR DRIVING UNDER THE INFLUENCE. Section 164(b)(1) of title 23, United States Code, is amended— (1) in subparagraph (A), by striking “ alcohol-impaired” and inserting “ alcohol- or multiple substance-impaired”; and (2) in subparagraph (B)— (A) by striking “ intoxicated or driving” and inserting “ intoxicated, driving while multiple substance-impaired, or driving”; and (B) by striking “ alcohol-impaired” and inserting “ alcohol- or multiple substance-impaired”.
