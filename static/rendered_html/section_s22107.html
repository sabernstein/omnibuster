SEC. 22107. AMTRAK OFFICE OF INSPECTOR GENERAL.   There are authorized to be appropriated to the Office of Inspector General of Amtrak the following amounts:(1) For fiscal year 2022, $26,500,000. (2) For fiscal year 2023, $27,000,000. (3) For fiscal year 2024, $27,500,000. (4) For fiscal year 2025, $28,000,000. (5) For fiscal year 2026, $28,500,000.
