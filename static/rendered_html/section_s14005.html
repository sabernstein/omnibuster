SEC. 14005. BUREAU OF INDIAN AFFAIRS ROAD MAINTENANCE PROGRAM.   There are authorized to be appropriated to the Director of the Bureau of Indian Affairs to carry out the road maintenance program of the Bureau— (1) $50,000,000 for fiscal year 2022; (2) $52,000,000 for fiscal year 2023; (3) $54,000,000 for fiscal year 2024; (4) $56,000,000 for fiscal year 2025; and (5) $58,000,000 for fiscal year 2026.
