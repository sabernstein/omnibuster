SEC. 13003. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT DATA SUPPORT PROGRAM.  Section 6028(c) of the FAST Act (23 U.S.C. 150 note; Public Law 114–94) is amended by striking “fiscal years 2016 through 2020” and inserting “fiscal years 2022 through 2026”.
