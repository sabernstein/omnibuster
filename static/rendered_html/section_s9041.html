SEC. 9041. EXTENSION OF LIMITATION ON EXCESS BUSINESS LOSSES OF NONCORPORATE TAXPAYERS. (a) In General.— Section 461(l)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking “ January 1, 2026” each place it appears and inserting “ January 1, 2027”. (b) Effective Date.— The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
