SEC. 9022. EXTENSION OF FULL FEDERAL FUNDING OF EXTENDED UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION. (a) In General.— Section 4105 of the Families First Coronavirus Response Act ( 26 U.S.C. 3304 note) is amended by striking “ March 14, 2021” each place it appears and inserting “ September 6, 2021”. (b) Effective Date.— The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply as if included in the enactment of the Families First Coronavirus Response Act ( Public Law 116–127).
