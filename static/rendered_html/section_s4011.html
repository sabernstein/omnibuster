SEC. 4011. APPROPRIATION FOR THE TECHNOLOGY MODERNIZATION FUND.   In addition to amounts otherwise appropriated, there is appropriated to the General Services Administration for fiscal year 2021, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $1,000,000,000, to remain available until September 30, 2025, to carry out the purposes of the Technology Modernization Fund.
