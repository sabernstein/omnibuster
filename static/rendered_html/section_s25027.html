SEC. 25027. GAO STUDY ON IMPROVING THE EFFICIENCY OF TRAFFIC SYSTEMS.   Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall carry out, and submit to Congress a report describing the results of, a study on the potential societal benefits of improving the efficiency of traffic systems.
