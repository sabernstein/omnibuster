SEC. 9014. EXTENSION OF FULL FEDERAL FUNDING OF THE FIRST WEEK OF COMPENSABLE REGULAR UNEMPLOYMENT FOR STATES WITH NO WAITING WEEK. (a) In General.— Section 2105(e)(2) of the CARES Act ( 15 U.S.C. 9024(e)(2)) is amended by striking “ March 14, 2021” and inserting “ September 6, 2021”. (b) Full Reimbursement.— Paragraph (3) of section 2105(c) of such Act ( 15 U.S.C. 9024(c)) is repealed and such section shall be applied to weeks of unemployment to which an agreement under section 2105 of such Act applies as if such paragraph had not been enacted. In implementing the preceding sentence, a State may, if necessary, reenter the agreement with the Secretary under section 2105 of such Act, and retroactively pay for the first week of regular compensation without a waiting week consistent with State law (including a waiver of State law) and receive full reimbursement for weeks of unemployment that ended after December 31, 2020.
